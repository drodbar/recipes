Ingredients:
 - 1 tablespoon sugar
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil
 - 1 pinch or salt
 - 1 egg
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup flour

Directions:
 1. Beat the egg and mix in all the ingredients together in a cup or a bowl.
 2. Heat up the pan and spill some of the mix once it is very hot. Turn over when the pancake chages color and leave it there for a few more seconds.
 
 3. Serve in a dish with syrup, cream, nutella, jam or what not!
